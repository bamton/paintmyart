## Phase 2: Technical Specifications

### Core Specifications
- **Trim Size:** 8.5" x 11" (US Letter) with 0.5" Safe Zone margins
- **Resolution:** 300 DPI for print quality
- **Color Mode:** Grayscale/Black and white for printer compatibility

### Line Weights
- **3pt (Heavy):** Main character outlines, borders, major shapes
- **1pt (Light):** Interior details (fur texture, clothing patterns), background elements
- **Dashed Lines (---):** STRICTLY for cutting instructions
- **Dotted Lines (...):** STRICTLY for folding or tracing instructions

### Font Strategy
- **Headings:** Bubble/Outline Font (allow kids to color the titles)
- **Body Text:** Varela Round or Fredoka One (Rounded, legible sans-serif)

### Printer Safety Rules
For pages 7, 27, 28, & 29 (Cut-outs), place a small disclaimer icon: "Best printed single-sided!"
